PLAYER RELATED TRIGGERS:

upkeep : Triggered during your upkeep
die : Triggered when you have a creature die
etb : Triggered when you have a creature etb
end : Triggered at end of your turn



OPPONENT RELATED TRIGGERS:

oppupkeep : Triggered during your opponent's upkeep
oppdie : Triggered when an opponent's creature dies
oppetb : Triggered when an opponent's creature etb
oppend : Triggered at end of opponent's turn
